## Start building on Shopier today
Welcome to the Shopier Developer Portal. Here you'll find comprehensive guides and documentation to help you start working with Shopier as quickly as possible.
Integrate with Shopier to support Shopier merchants and grow your app audience by publishing a listed app. Or simply build an unlisted app for your own or customer's needs. Follow these easy steps below to build an integration with us.

## ✍️ 1. Create an account
[Sign up](https://www.shopier.com/m/signup.php) for a Shopier seller account first. Then enable the developer mode to start creating your apps.

## 🛠 2. Build your app
Getting Started guide is the starting point to understand how to integrate with Shopier. On [API Reference](https://developer.shopier.com/reference/introduction) section you'll find a large library of APIs and webhooks to build and integrate quickly.

## 🚀 3. Launch your app
Submit your app for our review. Publish it to [Shopier App Store](https://appstore.shopier.com) or promote it only on your own website.

## Focus on merchants' needs
Shopier App Store is the destination for merchants with the need of additional features. Publish your app to the Shopier App Store to reach hundreds of thousands of Shopier merchants.
By accessing merchants' data via Shopier API, develop value added solutions such as,
➡️ digital marketing campaigns, ➡️ electronic invoicing and accounting integrations, ➡️ CRM and ERP integrations, ➡️ order automation & product automation solutions, ➡️ integrations with third party platforms, ➡️ customized reporting services.
